About La Film Freaks

La Film Freaks is a free website that you can freely read and review on any movies you like, anonimously or not. We provide large number of reviews, from amateur to professionals, allowing you to find the best one suitable for you. We hope, with our website, you can save your time and have the best cinematic time ever.

Read and Write Reviews

The Reviews are displayed on our main page, and any Movie pages you enter. Click on the Reviews will lead you to read that Review. Read reviews, upvotes if you agree with it, downvote if you don't. And depends on how much details the review gives out, rate the Spoil-o-meter!

The Add Review button appears everywhere. Click on it, find the movie you have the hype for, write the reviews either in video or text, based on the tools we provide, and start. Don't forget to rate your own movie' Spoil-o-meter, so that viewers that come to read your review can prapare.

Spoil-o-meter

The special meter determines whether a review is suitable for late viewers of movies or not. If the review is safe, about 50% below, anyone can read it. But if it is above 50%, be prepared for the upcoming details. The Spoil-o-meter appears for both writers and readers:

- The reader can judge by themselves how much spoiled the review is.

- The writer can rate their own review, so that appropriate readers can join and other late birds can avoid the review.

Ranking System

For each reviews you write, and for each reviews you comment on, you will receive points. The more points you have, you will advance to higher positions in ranking, making you a more critical critic. Your reviews and yourself will appear as bright as any actors, and welcomed by other readers. You will also have some special features that only a Super Critic can own.

There are four positions in our system:

- Rookie Reviewer: Starting Point

- Usual Watcher: 200 Points

- Deep Look: 500 Points

- Super Critic: 1000 Points

So how can I earn points? Simple, for each review you write, you receive one. But if that review is upvoted, commented, you get one more point for each upvote, and three points for the comment. But be careful, if you receive too much downvotes, your score gonna be deducted. And by commenting on others' review, if your comment is upvoted, you can also receive 1 point every 2 upvotes. That is it. Now, make your way to be the best.
